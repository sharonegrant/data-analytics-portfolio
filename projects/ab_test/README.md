# A/B Test Analysis

This project showcases the design and analysis of an A/B test comparing two versions of a product or feature.

## Contents

- `data/` – test results or links to experiment data
- `notebooks/` – a Jupyter notebook describing the experimental design, assumptions, statistical tests, and results
- `src/` – reusable scripts for statistical testing or simulation
- `reports/` – summary report or presentation slide deck

## Overview

1. **Problem Statement** – Define the objective of the A/B test and the metric(s) of interest.
2. **Experiment Design** – Detail the sample size, randomization, and duration assumptions.
3. **Analysis** – Apply appropriate statistical tests and calculate confidence intervals, p-values, and lift.
4. **Findings & Recommendations** – Communicate whether the test results justify adopting one variation over the other, and any recommendations.
5. **Limitations** – Discuss potential biases, uncontrolled factors, or external impacts.

Replace this template with your specific A/B test details and results.
